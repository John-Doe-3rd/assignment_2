why visit

Famous for its ancient history, gorgeous islands, sunny beaches, and warm hospitality — Greece blends culture, nature, and cuisine perfectly.

best time to visit

April-June and September-October — pleasant weather, fewer crowds, and great prices.

top attractions

  1. Athens: Acropolis and Parthenon

  2. Santorini: Iconic sunsets and cliffside villages

  3. Crete: Ancient Knossos and mountain landscapes

  4. Meteora: Monasteries perched on rock towers
